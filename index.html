About Us

D.P SOLAR was established by Dr. Ravi Parkash Sharma in 2018 with a mission to provide reliable and affordable solar energy solutions. Since then, the company has grown into a trusted name in the solar industry, offering a wide range of products including solar panels, inverters, batteries, water heaters, and lighting systems. With a focus on quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction, D.P SOLAR is committed to powering a greener future for homes and businesses across Punjab.
